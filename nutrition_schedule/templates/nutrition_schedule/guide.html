How to Use This App

Goto the Search Page, and enter the age, select the gender and then activity level of the child from the respective dropdown lists. Then click on the Submit Button.

A page with your results containing the list of suggested food items, generated at random will appear on the page. You can click on the print button on the top right side of the screen to print the result.
